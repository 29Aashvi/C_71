What's New

  • New owls have been trained as special messengers for the parents!

    New owls have been trained to inform parents as soon as news like, OWLs results, when the student leaves and when the student arrives comes out. This way parents will be informed of each and everything done by the ward and will recieve important information at the soonest. This new feature will prove to be handy!

  • Four new area has been added for quidditch practice for all four houses.

    All four houses need space to practice Quidditch for the competitions that take place in school, hence we have prepared four of these areas so all four houses can train with ease and whenever they like. This reduces the loss of practice and also avoids fights between both students and the head teachers of the houses.
